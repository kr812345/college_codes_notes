Aarti Shri Krishna Ki

(Verse 1)
Hail to Lord Krishna, the enchanting flutist, Whose divine melody echoes through creation. With eyes like lotus petals, radiant and deep, He captivates hearts in love's eternal sweep.
(Chorus)
Jai Shri Krishna, Jai Shri Krishna, Victory to Lord Krishna, the source of bliss. Jai Shri Krishna, Jai Shri Krishna, In devotion, we revel in your divine kiss.
(Verse 2)
Adorned with peacock feathers, the symbol of grace, Krishna, the cowherd, in every heart finds a place. His playful charm and mischievous smile, Guide us through life's journey, mile by mile.
(Chorus)
Jai Shri Krishna, Jai Shri Krishna, In your cosmic dance, the universe spins. Jai Shri Krishna, Jai Shri Krishna, In devotion's flame, our love begins.
(Verse 3)
Bearer of the Sudarshan Chakra, mighty and bright, Protector of dharma, dispelling the night. In the Bhagavad Gita, your wisdom unfolds, A timeless scripture, as the epic tale molds.
(Chorus)
Jai Shri Krishna, Jai Shri Krishna, In your divine presence, our souls take flight. Jai Shri Krishna, Jai Shri Krishna, Guiding us from darkness to eternal light.
(Verse 4)
As we offer this aarti with love so pure, May your blessings in our hearts endure. Jai Shri Krishna, the Supreme Being, In devotion, our souls find eternal meaning.
(Chorus)
Jai Shri Krishna, Jai Shri Krishna, With each flicker, our faith ignites. Jai Shri Krishna, Jai Shri Krishna, In your love, the universe unites.
